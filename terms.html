Passports and Visas

A valid passport is required for all international travel. It must be valid for at least six months from the date of your arrival back in your home country. Check your passport expiration date to be sure it meets this requirement, and if necessary, apply for a renewal as soon as possible. You will not be allowed to board your departure flight if your passport expiration date is within six months of your scheduled return to your home country.

You must also have a valid visa to enter most countries. If you need assistance with such requirements, African Connections recommends that you contact a visa service provider.

It is your responsibility to comply with all passport, visa, and other travel requirements of any country visited.

African Connections assumes no responsibility for travelers who fail to comply with the passport, visa, or other travel requirements of any country visited.
