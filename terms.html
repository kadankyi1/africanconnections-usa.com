Tour prices stated are per person and based on double occupancy and economy airfare from New York or the Washington D.C. area.. A single room upgrade may be available. Air Arrangements

In our commitment to keeping your tour costs low, while providing you the best tour experience, we take advantage of the promotional fares offered by several different airlines. This might result in our changing travel schedules and or changing listed airlines. However, African Connections will not change the airline or travel schedule within 30 days of your scheduled travel date.

Despite African Connections’ commitment not to change the travel schedule within 30 days of your scheduled travel date, international air schedules are subject to change at any time. If you are responsible for purchasing domestic tickets to connect with an international flight, you must be sure you arrange for a flight that will not compromise your on-time arrival for your international flight.

We recommend that you do not purchase domestic air tickets with high penalty charges for changes. We further recommend that you wait to purchase your domestic air ticket until 29 days before your departure.

We also recommend that you contact your airline at least 30 days prior to your departure to determine its pre-departure procedures and requirements for boarding and travel to your destination. You should further note that airline pre-departure procedures and requirement are subject to change at any time.

It is your responsibility to comply with all airline pre-departure procedures and requirements.

African Connections assumes no responsibility for travelers who fail to comply with airline pre-departure procedures and requirements.



Passports and Visas

A valid passport is required for all international travel. Your passport must be valid for at least six months from the date of your arrival back in your home country. Check your passport expiration date to be sure it meets this requirement and if necessary, apply for a renewal as soon as possible. You will not be allowed to board your departure flight if your passport expiration date is within six months of your scheduled return to your home country.

You also must also have a valid visa to entry must countries. If you need assistance concerning such requirements, African Connections recommends that you contact a visa service provider.

It is your responsibility to comply with all passport, visa and any other travel requirement of any country visited.

African Connections assumes no responsibility for travelers who fail to comply with passport, visa or any other travel requirement of any country visited.



Immunization and Medical Information

Travelers should frequently check the immunization requirements for each country that they plan to visit prior to departure because vaccination requirements may change.

It is your responsibility to comply with all immunization requirements.

African Connections assumes no responsibility for travelers who fail to comply with immunization requirements.

Your doctor might also recommend other vaccinations which are not mandatory for travel to the host country.

While it is not mandatory, it is very important for you acquire anti-malarial medication. Malaria is a serious potential (but treatable) health threat you should take necessary precautions to minimize your risk. It is also a good idea to bring your favorite mosquito repellant to further protect yourself from the mosquitos that you will encounter.
